Gestures Talk: Voice Through Gestures

Welcome to Gesture Talk, a revolutionary project that harnesses the power of hand gestures to enhance communication and connectivity. Our mission is to break down barriers and provide an inclusive platform for individuals to express themselves effortlessly.

What We Offer

1. Gesture Prediction: Gesture Talk allows users to input letters from A to Z using hand gestures. It predicts letters, words, and even small sentences in real-time, creating a natural communication experience.

2. Custom Gesture Training: We give users the power to train our system to recognize custom hand gestures. You can define unique gestures for specific actions, words, or phrases based on your needs.

3. Assistive Technology: Gesture Talk is designed to be a valuable assistive tool, especially in healthcare and medical scenarios. It can provide a means of communication for patients in environments where verbal communication may be challenging.

Who Can Benefit from Gesture Talk?

1. Communication for the Differently Abled:

- People with speech impairments can use custom gestures to communicate more effectively.

2. Healthcare and Medical Scenarios:

- Healthcare professionals can also use custom gestures to streamline processes and access information quickly.

3. Sign Language Interpretation:

- Gesture Talk can assist in bridging the gap between sign language users and non-sign language users by providing a common communication platform.

4. Custom sign language translation:

- Imagine a family that has a member with a rare medical condition that causes difficulties in vocal communication.
